### [Water Taxi](https://www.clearwaterlaketours.com/services.php)
The price includes a *reasonable amount of gear. The price to transport your own canoe or kayak is $30 plus 5% tax.

*A reasonable amount of gear for two people would be: 2 packs, 1 tent, 1 cooler and a medium storage box. If people have a large amount of gear an extra charge will be negotiated. **Prices are for one way.**
